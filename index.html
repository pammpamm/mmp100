Caroline

Caroline lives within a Magic Dimension, called Astoria. This universe is filled with different classes of species. Some of these species have powers, the majority of them residing in the city of Helen. Caroline has a very extroverted and spontaneous personality. She loves making friends and helping people whenever she gets the chance to. Caroline is a Willow, which are magical beings capable of manipulating the laws of nature of defying gravity, with the use of their power of telekinesis.
